Side Projects

My side projects include:

Seeing that students in my Japanese classes tended to stick together with people they knew rather than with different people every class, I made this tool to help my Japanese professors mix conversational practice groups without needing to think about who had worked with who else before. It is a basic website app that I am working on improving mostly design-wise. Some of the key methods I got experience with were the getItem() and setItem() functions from the localStorage API.
